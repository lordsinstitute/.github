# This Code of Conduct governs all interactions within the Lords Institute of Engineering & Technology GitHub Organization.

## 1. Purpose

The **Lords Institute of Engineering & Technology (LIET) GitHub Organization Code of Conduct** defines the standards of behavior expected from all students, faculty, staff, alumni, and collaborators contributing to repositories under the official LIET GitHub Organization.

This organization exists solely for **academic learning, research collaboration, institutional projects, and professional development**.  
All contributors are expected to uphold LIET’s values of integrity, professionalism, and respect.

## 2. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within:

- LIET GitHub repositories  
- Issues, pull requests, and discussions  
- Documentation and collaboration activities  
- Academic assignments, mini-projects, and research work  
- Official communication related to LIET GitHub repositories  

It applies to students, faculty, maintainers, reviewers, and external academic collaborators.


## 3. Core Values

All participants must uphold the following principles:

### Academic Integrity
- Submit only original work.
- Properly cite references and collaborators.
- Avoid plagiarism or misuse of academic content.

### Professionalism
- Communicate respectfully and constructively.
- Follow repository contribution guidelines.
- Fulfill assigned responsibilities on time.

### Respect & Inclusivity
- Respect individuals regardless of background, religion, gender, or skill level.
- Encourage collaborative learning.
- Support beginners and peers.

### Responsibility
- Protect institutional data and intellectual property.
- Maintain confidentiality when required.
- Use institutional resources ethically.

## 4. Expected Behavior

Participants should:

- Use respectful and professional language.
- Provide constructive technical feedback.
- Follow coding, documentation, and version-control standards.
- Respect repository maintainers’ decisions.
- Work honestly in team projects.
- Represent LIET professionally in public contributions.


## 5. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive remarks  
- Personal attacks, trolling, or public shaming  
- Plagiarism or academic dishonesty  
- Uploading confidential or unauthorized materials  
- Posting illegal, unethical, or inappropriate content  
- Intentionally disrupting academic collaboration  
- Misrepresenting institutional work as personal work  

Such behavior may result in warnings, removal from repositories, or institutional disciplinary action.

## 6. Centralized Reporting Process

Lords Institute of Engineering & Technology maintains a **centralized reporting system** to ensure fairness, confidentiality, and professional handling of all concerns.

If you experience or witness a violation of this Code of Conduct, report it to the official LIET GitHub Governance Team at:

### 📧 liet-gh@lords.ac.in  

### When Reporting, Please Include:
- Repository name and link  
- Description of the incident  
- Screenshots or evidence (if available)  
- Your contact details (optional if anonymous reporting is allowed)

### Reporting Assurance
All reports will be:
- Handled confidentially  
- Reviewed by authorized faculty and institute representatives  
- Investigated professionally and fairly  

Retaliation against anyone reporting a concern is strictly prohibited.


## 7. Getting Help with a Violation — TL;DR

If at any time you feel **unsafe, unwelcome, harassed, or uncomfortable** due to an interaction within the LIET GitHub Organization, please immediately contact:

📧 **liet-gh@lords.ac.in**

Your report will be treated **confidentially, respectfully, and professionally**.  
For urgent safety concerns, please also contact your Department Office or Institute Administration.


## 8. Governance Committee

The LIET GitHub Organization is governed by an authorized committee that may include:

- Faculty Coordinator(s)  
- Department Representatives  
- Repository Maintainers  
- Institute Administration Representative  

The committee is responsible for reviewing reports and ensuring enforcement of this Code.


## 9. Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Warning or guidance  
- Removal of content  
- Suspension of repository access  
- Academic disciplinary action as per LIET policy  

Decisions will be made fairly, with the goal of maintaining a respectful academic environment.


## 10. Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by globally accepted open-source collaboration standards and adapted specifically for academic use at Lords Institute of Engineering & Technology.


## 11. Commitment

By participating in the LIET GitHub Organization, all contributors agree to uphold this Code of Conduct and help maintain a respectful, ethical, and professional academic environment.
